Travel

Travelling is one of the most important elements of my life, it's actually the reason why I'm currently studying engineering. The mere action of visiting places that I have never visited creates a sensation of self-fulfillment that disconnects me completely from the issues of reality, this sensation is so intense that makes me want to live in every place I visit.
Now I'm going to share my experiences so you can feel the same way that I do every time I travel.
